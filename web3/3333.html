    2.

    Suppose we want to find the instantaneous rate of change of \(f(x)=x^{2}+5x\) at \(x=3\text{:}\)
    1. Circle the correct formula for calculating the instantaneous rate of change of \(f\) at \(x = 3\text{:}\)
      \begin{equation*} m = \lim_{h \to 3}\frac{f(3+h)-f(3)}{h}\quad \quad \text{OR}\quad \quad m = \lim_{h \to 0}\frac{f(3+h)-f(3)}{h} \end{equation*}
      🔗
      🔗
    2. Follow the steps below to compute \(m\text{.}\)
      1. Calculate \(f(3+h)\) and combine like terms.
        🔗
        🔗
      2. Calculate \(f(3+h)-f(3)\) and combine like terms.
        🔗
        🔗
      3. Calculate the difference quotient \(\frac{f(3+h)-f(3)}{h}\) and simplify to get rid of \(h\) on the bottom.
        🔗
        🔗
      4. Take the limit \(h \to 0\) of your simplified expression in iii. Label the resulting value as \(m\text{.}\)
        🔗
        🔗
